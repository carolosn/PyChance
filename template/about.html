About PyChance Casino

PyChance is a fictional online casino created as a class project to practice Python, Flask, and web development concepts.

The goal of this site is to simulate a simple gaming platform where users can navigate between pages, view different games, manage a wallet, and access help and contact information just like a real website.

No real money is used on this site. All funds and bets are simulated for educational purposes only.
